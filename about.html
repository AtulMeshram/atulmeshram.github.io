A little about me.

I'm a designer and programmer born and raised in the suburbs of Bastar, currently live in the Bengaluru.

I'm driven by a fascination with aesthetic, delightful, and intuitive products. The satisfying "click of a well-made box".

I have skills and experiences spanning design and engineering that allow me to take ideas from concept to mock to prototype to, ultimately, functional end products.

Outside of technology, I enjoy biking, traveling, exploring the world, and clicking photos. I also spend some of my time with books and put my emotions into poetry.

Interests

Currently, I’m particularly interested in immersive computing and XR, spatial interfaces, emerging technologies, and creative applications of technology in the social sector.

I'm always happy to chat.
