ABOUT PROJECT

This project is based on conceptual product design.
Inspired by true wireless stereo headset technology.
For the 'Love of Music'.

PRODUCT

Inspired by Crazybaby products.
Product illustration is true vector graphic design.
Illustration gives context about product and supporting app.

APP UI

All app screens are mockup for conceptual Air app.
App graphics and User Interface (UI) is optimised
to give best user experience for device accessibility and
customisable music experience.

FEATURES

Air is the first true wireless in-ear headphone in the world to implement carbon nanotube technology. This is a major industry breakthrough that will allow Air to have a comfortable, ergonomic design while looking, feeling and, most importantly, sounding spectacular.
